# Architecture Comparison Templates

## Three-Option Structure

### Option 1: Minimal Modernization
**Goal**: Incremental improvements with minimal risk
**Approach**: Address critical issues only
**Timeline**: 1-3 months
**Cost**: Low
**Best For**: Limited budget, risk-averse teams

### Option 2: Moderate Modernization
**Goal**: Balanced improvements across key areas
**Approach**: Strategic refactoring + new features
**Timeline**: 3-6 months
**Cost**: Medium
**Best For**: Most teams, balanced risk/reward

### Option 3: Full Modernization
**Goal**: Complete architecture overhaul
**Approach**: Greenfield redesign with best practices
**Timeline**: 6-12 months
**Cost**: High
**Best For**: Long-term investment, severe tech debt
